About our process

1.

Prep:

We begin with a rinsless wash to remove loose dirt, bugs and grime. Then we prep the exterior of the car with a clay bar and lubricant to decontaminate the surface of the paint.

2.

Decontaminate:

We treat the exterior of the car with a clay bar and lubricant to decontaminate the surface from pollutants in the air that attach to the surface of the the paint.

3.

Wax:

Once the surface is prepared, we apply a wax coating made form Carnuba, which gives your car's surface a deep glossy shine.
