Exterior

Wash & Wax

Need a car wash? Relax, we'll bring it to you. If your car just requires a wash to remove dust from every day driving, our no-rinse gentle hand wash, panel by panel, to remove dust and contaminants. Bugs, pollen, and other debris are carefully removed without agitating the clear coat. For the second step, we apply a wax to restore your finish to it's natural shine and add a protective coating.

Exterior

Polish & Protectant

We begin with the same process as our standard wash. Then use a clay bar and contaminant remover to prep your car for the polishing step. Unlike a traditional car wash or wash and wax, which breaks down under normal conditions and warm temperatures, our sealants maintain surface shine for months. Our method removes the most severe oxidation and corrosion and restores your finish to its original shine. Finally, we add a wax sealant to add additional shine to restore your vehilce's finish to showroom quality.

Interior

Complete Interior Detailing

Messy interior? It happens. We have kids, jobs, busy lives. We eat food inside our cars and we dont always have time to clean up. We go to beaches, parks and places where we track sand and dirt inside the car. While you relax, your dash, center console and door panels will be cleaned and restored like new. Floors and mats vacuumed and shampooed, stains and discolorations removed. An added protectant will protect and keep your interior safe from further staining.

Exterior

Headlight Restoration

More than a cosmetic issue, clouded lenses can pose a serious threat to safety, compromising night vision and reducing the effectiveness of your headlights by up to 80 percent. Our headlight lens restoration process can bring your lenses back to near-factory condition and save you money on new headlilght lenses.
