OUR SERVICES

Full service auto detailing, color restoration and protection.

Located in Orange County CA, we will evaluate your car to determine your needs and the best course of action. We will customize a price plan that works for you to get the results you're looking for. Let us do the dirty work to restore every part of your car from the wheels, tires, headlights, windows, rugs, floormats and engine.

Exterior

Polish & Protectant

Unlike traditional wash and wax which breaks down under normal conditions and warm temperatures, our sealants maintain surface shine for months. Out method removes the most severe oxidation and corrosion and restores your finish to its original shine.

Exterior

No-rinse hand wash

If your car just requires a wash to remove dust from every day driving, our no-rinse gentle hand wash uses a light water rinse with a Carnuba wax coating to quickly restore your finish to it's natural shine.

Interior

Upholstery & Leather

I can remove stains and discoloration from years of abuse, to near original condition. An added protectant will protect and keep your seats safe from further staining.
