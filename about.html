ABOUT US

Full service mobile auto detailing, from bumber to bumper

MPH Detail uses a virtually waterless process that doesn't involve power washers or noisy generators. Using a low-noise oscillating polisher that is safe for the surface of your car we can restore your car to a near showroom quality. Our process is eco-friendly we don't waste water or put harmful cleansers or degreasers into the ground.

WHAT WE DO

Delivering the results you expect

Unlike traditional wash and wax which breaks down under normal conditions and warm temperatures, our sealants maintain surface shine for months.

HOW WE DO IT

Restore your cars finish and interior

Our products are made to maintain interiors that are under constant abuse. Our process restores the beauty and druability to your cars carpeting and upholstry.
